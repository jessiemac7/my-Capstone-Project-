About Us

An "Italy Restaurant" that serves italian cuisine, which is known for using fresh Ingredients like olive oil, tomatoes, basil, and cheeses to create dishes like pasta, pizza and seafood. The cuisine are based on the mediterranean diet which features fresh Ingredients like garlic etc. One of our trait is the commitment to making handmade pasta and freshly prepared sauces

For an authentic Italian experience with pasta and lasagna, consider visiting "Selve" for its family dining or single dining in Rome. We provide an atmospheric and exclusive experience. Selve specialises in classic and unique flavors when making each dishes. Prices offered are affordable with a fine dining experience. Lasagna is a sheet pasta, made up of flat, thin rectangules of dough.
